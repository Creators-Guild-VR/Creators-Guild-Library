A tiling texture is an image texture that when repeated across a mesh matches itself perfectly edge to edge. 

This is useful because sometimes we want to texture something far too large to practically use a unique texture.

The downside of a tiling texture is that you do not typically have the ability to fit the texture to the geometry by painting on details. 

There are some exceptions to this, by using [[Layered Texture Blending]] or [[Trim Sheets]]. Which use tiling textures in different ways.
