 In order to calculate Turn angle, take off in your plane, when you hit the desired speed, pull horizontally with your flaps deployed. Take the maximum amount of G and speed here and use this equation to calculate your estimated turn angle
 
(Gs/air speed) X 1091  
= Degrees per second

  
Example:  
10g at 400knots 10/400 = .025  
.025 x 1091 = 27.275 degrees per second
